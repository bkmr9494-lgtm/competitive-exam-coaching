Frequently Asked Questions

Got Questions? We Have Answers

What courses do you offer?

We offer comprehensive coaching for UPSC, SSC, Banking, Railway, Defence, State PCS, CUET, NDA, and CDS examinations. Each course includes complete syllabus coverage, test series, and interview guidance.

What is the fee structure?

Our fee structure varies based on the course and duration. We offer flexible payment options including EMI. Please contact our admissions team for detailed fee information.

Do you provide online classes?

Yes, we provide both online and offline classes. Our online classes are live interactive sessions with the same quality as classroom teaching. You also get access to recorded lectures.

Is there any scholarship available?

Yes, we conduct scholarship tests regularly. Based on your performance, you can get up to 100% scholarship. We also have merit-based scholarships for deserving students.

How can I book a demo class?

You can book a free demo class by filling the contact form on our website or calling our admissions team. Demo classes are available for all courses.

Do you provide study material?

Yes, we provide comprehensive study material including books, notes, practice papers, and current affairs updates. The material is regularly updated to match the latest exam pattern.
